**Reaching customers socially** and bringing the virtual world to reality. Keeping the conversations top of mind with current retail store locations, products available, places to dine, and events. Engaging with customers online keeps our client top of mind in the virtual social community and reminds real customers that Palladio in Folsom is the place they love to shop, dine, and play.

Create engaging branded content and digital advertising across social media to promote the Palladio Mall, its retailers, and event partners as the premier destination to Shop, Dine & Play.

Expand user engagement online with special events such as Comic Con, the Summer Concert Series, and special announcements. The branded messaging has increased foot traffic at the brick-and-mortar stores and attendance at the many featured events.
